How effective is billboard advertising?

Adding out of home media, and billboard advertising to your media plan can significantly increase your reach into a market, and your ability to connect to your target audience. While other media platforms are losing traction do to fragmentation of advertising channels, out of home is thriving due largely to its time tested ability to deliver high visibility, cost effective programs. Out of home is also ideally suited to work in synchronicity with mobile platforms and social media.

When OOH is added to a media plan, it can increase reach of the overall campaign:

  • • TV campaigns by 18%
  • • Radio campaigns by 45%
  • • Web campaigns by 68%
  • • Social campaigns by 212%

Source: Touchpoints USA Billboard Study, 2012

7 out of 10 Adults have seen an OOH ad in the past month, and out of those 18+ who have seen an OOH ad:

  • • 41% are more likely to learn about the brand being advertised.
  • • 55% use mobile/social media to share information while viewing an out-of-home ad campaign.
  • • 70% say out-of-home is very or somewhat likely to influence a purchase.
  • • 58% search the web as a direct result of seeing an ad.

Source: Touchpoints USA Billboard Study, 2012

Understanding how billboards are measured:

Since 2010 the measurement of outdoor advertising has undergone a revolution. Gone are the days when billboard advertising had its own completely unique (and often confusing) system of measurement. With the introduction of Out of Home Ratings (OOHR) billboards can now be measured in media currency that is consistent with other channels like TV, radio and print. How much does billboard advertising typically cost?

The costs for billboard advertising can vary greatly depending on the market, the media, and the locations involved. In other words Ottumwa, IA is not going to cost the same as Times Square. Given the wide range of variables, an out of home plan can range from $5,000 to $500,000. But don't worry, we can help. With a few details - like markets, targets and timing - we can supply you with some realistic planning rates to get started.
